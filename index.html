Writemind Academy FAQs



  • question_answerWhat kind of programs does writemind offer?
    Writemind offers creative writing programs like story structures, novel writing, script writing, etc It also conducts courses on SWOT analysis, time management, maximising productivity, etc.
  • question_answerWhat is the minimum and maximum time period of the courses?
    Minimum of 3 hours and maximum of one year.
  • question_answerAre the courses online or offline?
    Writemind Academy offers online, offline and hybrid programs.
  • question_answerWhat is the minimum qualification to join any course?
    Minimum qualification would be the ability to read and write. For some specialised courses there may be other criteria. To know more about this contact us here.
  • question_answerIn which language are the courses conducted?
    Courses are conducted in English, Marathi, Hindi and Konkani, depending on the mother tongue of the participants.


Company Bio

Writemind Academy is an initiative by writer journalist Ibrahim Afghan, to train and empower people to share their stories. He believes that every story needs to be told and everyone has the right to share their experiences with the world.
